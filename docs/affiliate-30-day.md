# High-Value Affiliate Categories

These categories tend to offer higher average order values because buyers often purchase multiple items together:

* Ergonomic office furniture
* Standing desks
* Office chairs
* Printers and scanners
* Monitor arms
* Mechanical keyboards
* Office lighting
* Storage and organization systems
* Productivity accessories
* Home office bundles

As your site grows, you can expand beyond general office supplies into specialized content silos such as:

* Ergonomic workspaces
* Remote work setups
* Small business office equipment
* Student study spaces
* Executive office furniture
* Gaming and hybrid workstations

This 30-day plan gives you **30 evergreen articles** that can continue attracting search traffic year-round while covering a broad range of office supply topics with strong affiliate potential.

For a new office supply affiliate site, SEO is less about "adding SEO" and more about creating a site that answers the questions people are already searching for. The first 3–6 months are usually spent building topical authority around a specific niche.

## Step 1: Pick a Narrow Niche

Instead of trying to rank for "office supplies," choose a focused area.

Examples:

* Home office setups
* Remote work equipment
* Office organization
* Desk accessories
* Small business office supplies
* Student office essentials
* Ergonomic office products

A niche helps search engines understand what your website specializes in.

# Step 6: Internal Linking

Every article should link to related content.

For example, an article about standing desks might link to:

* Ergonomic office chairs
* Monitor arms
* Anti-fatigue mats
* Cable management
* Home office setup guide

This helps visitors explore your site and helps search engines discover your pages.

# Step 7: On-Page SEO

For every article:

* Include the primary keyword in the title.
* Write a clear meta description.
* Use one H1 heading.
* Organize sections with H2 and H3 headings.
* Add descriptive image alt text.
* Use short, readable URLs.
* Keep paragraphs concise.

Example URL:

```cURL
officeloadouts.com
```

# Step 8: Add Helpful Content

Not every post should be a product roundup.

Examples:

* How to organize a small office
* Standing desk posture guide
* Remote work productivity tips
* Office cable management ideas
* Minimalist desk setup ideas

## A realistic timeline

* **Month 1:** Build the site structure and publish 20–30 high-quality articles.
* **Months 2–3:** Expand to 40–60 articles, improve internal linking, and start earning backlinks.
* **Months 4–6:** Refresh older posts, target more competitive keywords, and monitor which articles are gaining traffic.
* **Months 6–12:** Continue publishing consistently, optimize top-performing pages, and build authority in your chosen niche.

The key is consistency. A site with 75–100 well-organized, genuinely helpful articles focused on office supplies is much more likely to rank than a site with a handful of broad, generic posts.

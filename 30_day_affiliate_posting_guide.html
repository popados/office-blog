30-day affiliate blog posting guide divided into four weekly phases

30-day affiliate posting guide

A structured roadmap to launch, grow, and optimize your affiliate blog content.

Week 1 — Foundation (Days 1–7)

Set up your affiliate accounts, research your niche, and publish your first anchor content before promoting anything.

Goal: 2 posts live + affiliate links approved
1

Niche audit & affiliate signup

Strategy

  • Confirm niche: narrow it to one problem you solve
  • Sign up for 3–5 affiliate programs (Amazon, ShareASale, or niche-specific)
  • Bookmark affiliate dashboards & note approval times
Tip: prioritize programs with 30-day+ cookies
2

Keyword research sprint

SEO

  • Find 10 low-competition buyer-intent keywords
  • Identify 3 "best [product]" or "vs." comparison angles
  • Create a content calendar spreadsheet
3

Write your cornerstone review

Writing

  • Write a 1,500–2,000 word in-depth product review
  • Include pros/cons, real-use details, and a verdict
  • Add affiliate disclosure at the top
4

Publish & on-page SEO

Publishing

  • Publish review with optimized title, meta description, H2s
  • Add alt text to all images
  • Insert affiliate links naturally (3–5 placements)
5

Write a "best of" roundup

Writing

  • Write a "Best [X] for [Audience]" listicle (5–7 picks)
  • Link to your Day 3 review within the roundup
  • Draft a CTA button for each product
High conversion format
6

Publish roundup + internal links

Publishing

  • Publish roundup post
  • Link back and forth between roundup and your review
  • Submit both URLs to Google Search Console
7

Week 1 review & prep

Strategy

  • Confirm affiliate links are tracking correctly
  • Check affiliate program approval statuses
  • Plan 4 more content topics for Week 2

Week 2 — Content blitz (Days 8–14)

Build content velocity with comparison posts, how-tos, and trust-building pieces that support your affiliate articles.

Goal: 4 more posts live + email list started
8

Write a comparison post

Writing

  • Write "Product A vs Product B" (1,200+ words)
  • Include a comparison table near the top
  • End with a clear winner recommendation + affiliate link
High buyer intent
9

Publish comparison + setup email

Publishing

  • Publish comparison post with all affiliate links
  • Set up a free email tool (Kit, Mailchimp, etc.)
  • Create a simple opt-in lead magnet (checklist or guide)
10

Write a how-to tutorial

Writing

  • Write a step-by-step tutorial using an affiliated product
  • Include screenshots or images to boost dwell time
  • End with a "Get started with [Product]" CTA
11

Publish tutorial + social snippets

Publishing

  • Publish tutorial post
  • Create 2–3 social media snippets (quote cards, tips)
  • Share to Pinterest or relevant Facebook groups
12

Trust & authority post

Writing

  • Write an "About" or "My Experience With X" post
  • Share personal use case or results with the niche
  • This is non-affiliate — builds reader trust
Builds long-term credibility
13

Publish + add opt-in forms

Publishing

  • Publish trust post
  • Add email opt-in form to your top 2–3 posts
  • Set up a welcome email sequence (2–3 emails)
14

Week 2 review

Strategy

  • Check Google Search Console for early impressions
  • Review affiliate dashboards for click data
  • Plan Week 3 promotion & outreach targets

Week 3 — Promotion (Days 15–21)

Stop publishing and start promoting. Drive traffic to your existing content through backlinks, social, and community outreach.

Goal: 3 backlinks + 200+ pageviews this week
15

Guest post outreach

Outreach

  • Find 5 blogs in your niche accepting guest posts
  • Send personalized pitches with 2–3 topic ideas each
  • Check "write for us" pages and editorial guidelines
16

Write a new SEO post

Writing

  • Write a new post targeting a different buyer keyword
  • Add FAQ schema-friendly questions and answers
  • Publish and interlink with existing content
17

Reddit & forum visibility

Promotion

  • Find 3 relevant subreddits or forums in your niche
  • Leave genuinely helpful comments (no spam linking)
  • Post value-first content with a link if rules allow
Comment-first, link second
18

Pinterest & visual SEO

Promotion

  • Create 2–3 Pinterest pins for your best posts
  • Write keyword-rich pin descriptions
  • Join 1–2 group boards in your niche
19

Broken link building

Outreach

  • Use Ahrefs or free tools to find broken links in your niche
  • Email site owners offering your content as a replacement
  • Target 5–10 prospects per outreach batch
20

Social media scheduling

Promotion

  • Schedule 1 week of posts across 1–2 social platforms
  • Mix content types: tips, polls, behind-the-scenes
  • Include a link to a post in every 3rd–4th update
21

Week 3 traffic review

Strategy

  • Check Google Analytics for traffic sources
  • Note which posts have the highest engagement
  • Plan Week 4 optimization targets based on data

Week 4 — Optimize (Days 22–30)

Use real data to double down on what's working. Improve your top posts, test CTAs, and build repeatable monthly processes.

Goal: 1st affiliate commission + repeatable workflow
22

Improve your top post

Optimization

  • Identify your most-visited post so far
  • Add 300–500 more words addressing reader questions
  • Test a new headline and update publish date
Updating > publishing new
23

CTA & conversion audit

Optimization

  • Check affiliate click-through rates in your dashboard
  • Rewrite weak CTAs ("check price" → "see today's deal")
  • Add a sticky or in-content CTA button to top posts
24

Write a seasonal or trending post

Writing

  • Find a trending topic in your niche (Google Trends, Reddit)
  • Write a timely 1,000-word post while it's hot
  • Promote immediately on all channels
Timely = fast traffic
25

Email list nurture

Email

  • Send your first broadcast email to subscribers
  • Share your best post + one product recommendation
  • Ask one question to boost reply rate and learn needs
26

Speed & UX audit

Technical

  • Run your top post through PageSpeed Insights
  • Compress images over 200KB
  • Check mobile layout on your phone for readability
27

Add schema markup

Technical SEO

  • Add Review schema to product review posts
  • Add FAQ schema to how-to and comparison posts
  • Validate with Google's Rich Results Test
28

Write a resource/tools page

Writing

  • Create a "Tools I Use" or "Resources" page
  • List 8–12 products with your affiliate links + brief notes
  • Link to this page from your nav and top posts
Passive traffic earner
29

Competitor content gap analysis

Strategy

  • Review 2–3 competitors' top posts in your niche
  • Identify angles or products they haven't covered
  • Add 5 new topic ideas to your content calendar
30

30-day review & Month 2 plan

Strategy

  • Review: total posts, clicks, commissions, email subs
  • Identify your 2 best-performing posts to double down on
  • Set Month 2 targets: posts, traffic, and revenue goals
Milestone: month 1 complete

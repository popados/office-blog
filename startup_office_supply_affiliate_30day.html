30-day affiliate posting schedule for a startup office supply blog, organized into four weekly phases

Week 1 — Foundation (days 1–7)

Niche down to the startup office buyer, join affiliate programs, and publish two anchor posts before any promotion.

Goal: 2 posts live + affiliate programs approved
1

Affiliate program signup

Strategy

  • Sign up: Amazon Associates, Staples, Office Depot, Autonomous, FlexiSpot
  • Note cookie duration and commission rate for each
  • Bookmark affiliate dashboards and set up link tracking
Autonomous & FlexiSpot pay 5–8% on standing desks
2

Buyer persona + keyword research

SEO / strategy

  • Define persona: seed-stage founder, 5–20 person team, budget-conscious
  • Find 15 buyer-intent keywords: "best standing desk for startup", "home office chair under $400"
  • Build a 30-slot content calendar spreadsheet
3

Write cornerstone review

Writing

  • Write "Best standing desks for startups 2024" — 2,000 words, 5–7 picks
  • Include a comparison table near the top
  • Add affiliate disclosure above the fold
High AOV = high commissions
4

Publish + on-page SEO

Publishing

  • Publish with optimized title tag, meta description, H2 structure
  • Add alt text to all product images
  • Submit URL to Google Search Console
5

Write "office setup on a budget" guide

Writing

  • Write "How to furnish a 10-person startup office for under $5k"
  • Link to your standing desk roundup within the piece
  • Include a total cost breakdown table
Shareable in startup Slack groups
6

Publish budget guide + interlink

Publishing

  • Publish and cross-link both posts
  • Set up Google Analytics 4 and verify tracking
  • Create a "Resources" page placeholder with your top picks
7

Week 1 audit

Strategy

  • Confirm all affiliate links are firing correctly
  • Check program approval statuses
  • Draft 4 content titles for week 2

Week 2 — Content build (days 8–14)

Stack comparison posts, how-tos, and trust-building content. Start your email list. Hit 6 posts total by end of week.

Goal: 4 more posts + email opt-in live
8

Chair comparison post

Writing

  • Write "Herman Miller Aeron vs Steelcase Leap — startup office review"
  • Head-to-head table: price, warranty, lumbar, aesthetics
  • End with a clear pick based on team size and budget
Chairs = high AOV, high commission
9

Publish chair post + email setup

Publishing

  • Publish with all affiliate links and disclosure
  • Set up Kit or Mailchimp — free tier is fine
  • Create lead magnet: "Startup office checklist" PDF (1 page)
10

How-to: remote team home office stipend guide

Writing

  • Write "How to set up a $1,000 home office stipend policy for your team"
  • Embed recommended products per budget tier with affiliate links
  • Include a downloadable policy template CTA
HR angle = highly shareable
11

Publish stipend guide + social snippets

Publishing

  • Publish post and add email opt-in form mid-content
  • Write 3 LinkedIn snippets extracted from the post
  • Share to r/startups and r/remotework (value-first, no spam)
12

Trust post: "What we actually use"

Writing

  • Write a personal "here's our actual setup and why" post — no fluff
  • Include real photos if possible — authenticity drives E-E-A-T
  • Non-promotional tone; links are incidental
Builds the author credibility Google wants
13

Publish trust post + opt-in forms

Publishing

  • Publish and add email opt-in to top 3 posts
  • Write 2-email welcome sequence: intro + top picks
  • Add author bio with credentials to all posts
14

Week 2 data check

Strategy

  • Check GSC for early impressions and click data
  • Note which affiliate links are getting clicks
  • Plan week 3 outreach targets

Week 3 — Promotion (days 15–21)

Shift from publishing to distributing. Startup founders hang out on LinkedIn, Reddit, and Hacker News — go there.

Goal: 3 backlinks + 300+ pageviews this week
15

LinkedIn content strategy

Promotion

  • Post a "5 things we learned furnishing our startup office" thread
  • Tag 2–3 relevant founders or office managers
  • Link to your budget guide in the first comment (not the post body)
LinkedIn first comment = better organic reach
16

Write new SEO post: monitor roundup

Writing

  • Write "Best monitors for developers at a startup" — 5 picks
  • Include ultrawide and dual-monitor setups
  • Publish and interlink with existing posts
17

Hacker News + community outreach

Promotion

  • Post "Ask HN: what's your startup's WFH stipend policy?" — no link
  • Engage genuinely in comments for 48 hours
  • Share your guide only if someone directly asks for a resource
HN rewards participation over promotion
18

Guest post outreach

Outreach

  • Identify 5 startup ops or productivity blogs accepting guest posts
  • Pitch: "How to build an ergonomic office policy for a remote-first team"
  • Send personalized pitches — one topic per blog
19

Product roundup: keyboards & peripherals

Writing

  • Write "Best mechanical keyboards for engineers at startups" — 6 picks
  • Segment by budget: under $100, $100–200, $200+
  • Publish and share to r/MechanicalKeyboards with honest context
20

Pinterest + social scheduling

Promotion

  • Create 3 Pinterest pins for your office setup guides
  • Schedule 1 week of LinkedIn posts in Buffer or Hypefury
  • Mix: tips, questions, behind-the-scenes, link posts (1 in 4)
21

Week 3 traffic review

Strategy

  • Check GA4 for top traffic sources this week
  • Identify highest-engaged posts by time on page
  • Plan week 4 optimization targets from the data

Week 4 — Optimize (days 22–30)

Use real data to improve what's working. Upgrade CTAs, add schema, build your resources page, and set up month 2.

Goal: 1st commission + repeatable monthly workflow
22

Improve your top post

Optimization

  • Find your most-visited post in GA4
  • Add 400 words addressing questions from Reddit or comments
  • A/B test a new headline and update the publish date
Updating beats publishing new for quick wins
23

CTA + conversion audit

Optimization

  • Check affiliate click-through rates across all posts
  • Rewrite weak CTAs: "check price" → "see today's deal at Autonomous"
  • Add a sticky sidebar or in-content CTA button to top posts
24

Trending post: AI tools for startup ops

Writing

  • Write "The AI-powered startup office setup in 2024" — timely angle
  • Tie in office hardware (monitors, webcams) with AI workflow
  • Promote immediately across all channels
AI angle = high shareability in startup circles
25

Email broadcast

Email

  • Send first broadcast to your list with your 3 best posts
  • Feature one product recommendation with affiliate link
  • Ask: "What's the biggest pain in your office setup?" — boosts replies
26

Add schema markup

Technical SEO

  • Add Review + Product schema to all product review posts
  • Add FAQ schema to comparison and how-to posts
  • Validate all with Google's Rich Results Test
Unlocks star ratings in search results
27

Build your "startup toolkit" resources page

Writing

  • Create a "Our recommended startup office gear" page
  • Segment by category: desks, chairs, monitors, peripherals, storage
  • Link from nav and within all existing posts
Passive earner — ranks for "startup office gear"
28

Core Web Vitals + speed audit

Technical SEO

  • Run all posts through PageSpeed Insights
  • Compress any image over 150KB to WebP
  • Check mobile rendering on a real phone
29

Competitor gap analysis

Strategy

  • Review top 3 competing blogs for uncovered angles
  • Look for missing: niche startup roles (devs, designers, remote ops)
  • Add 8 new topic ideas to your content calendar
30

30-day review + month 2 plan

Strategy

  • Tally: posts live, total clicks, commissions earned, email subscribers
  • Identify your 2 best-performing posts to build clusters around
  • Set month 2 targets: 12 posts, 1,000 pageviews, first $50 commission
Month 1 complete
